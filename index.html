About The Program

The Women Techsters Fellowship is a comprehensive 1-year program divided into two phases: the first 6 months focuses on in-depth technical training, while the last 6 months provides practical work experience that includes opportunities for internships, freelancing, entrepreneurship, and mentoring. This ensures that participants gain real-world experience and can apply their newly acquired skills in real world scenarios.
